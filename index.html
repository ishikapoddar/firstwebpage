Joint Entrance Examination(JEE)

What is it?

In simple words it can be said as a gateway to the top Engineering Colleges of India. It consists of two examinations JEE MAIN and JEE ADVANCED, of about 10 to 12 lakhs students who give JEE MAIN 2.5 lakhs students qualify for JEE ADVANCED. JEE MAIN is conducted by NATIONAL TESTING AGENCY whereas JEE ADVANCED is conducted by any of the IITs. Students prepare for over 2 to 4 years of time for this examination. JEE ADVANCED is considered as one of the toughest examination in the world.

One can know much about it from JEE MAIN and JEE ADVANCED websites
